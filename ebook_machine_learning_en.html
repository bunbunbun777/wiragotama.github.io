Introduction to Machine Learning and Deep Learning (English Translation)

A free e-book by Jan Wira Gotama Putra. Please download the book (as a whole) here (Last Update: 11 Maret 2018).


This page is dedicated to my personal side project, which is writing an introduction to machine learning and deep learning book in Bahasa Indonesia (my mother tongue). This book aims to serve as an introductory book for Indonesian undergrads. This book absolutely cannot be used as a primary reference for studying machine learning since it contains only introductory-level materials (I believe a book should provide advanced-level materials as well to serve as a primary reference). Even so, we hope this book can help you in its own way (e.g., additional materials). At the very least, it recommends you some great machine learning references. This book is free, and you may download or share it for non-commercial purposes. Since this book isn't perfect, you might criticise, comment or suggest some improvements. Please feel free to email me.

Just a little bit story, I write this book to be able contributing to society. I have been writing this since October 2016, and as of March 2018, I have written 189 pages! It has been viewed more than 1500 times (as of 03 May 2018) based on the count from my blog and research gate alone (I don't know how many this page contributes). Even though the content of this book might not up to your standard/taste, I believe some people receive some benefits since it is written in a semi-formal language.

I would like to appreciate people who contributed to this project up till now (affiliation as of May 2018): Adhiguna Surya Kuncoro (Oxford/Google Deep Mind), Arief Yudha Satria (Bukalapak), Candy Olivia Muwalim (JAIST), Chairuni Aulia Nusapati (Traveloka), Genta Indra Winata (HKUST), Hayyu Luthfi Hanifah (Traveloka), I Gede Mahendra Darmawiguna (Ganesha University of Education) and Tifani Warnita (Tokyo Tech).
