Bio

My name is Jan Wira Gotama Putra. I am a PhD student at Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing Laboratory (自然言語処理研究室), Department of Computer Science (情報工学系), School of Computing (情報理工学院), Tokyo Institute of Technology (東京工業大学). I am currently working on text coherence, supervised by Prof. Takenobu Tokunaga. Before assuming my current topic, I worked on text summarization research. Besides those two topics, I am also interested in information retrieval and machine learning.
