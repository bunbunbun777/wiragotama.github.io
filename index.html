Research

I am currently working on argumentative writing support system for L2 students, supervised by Prof. Takenobu Tokunaga. My research aims to help English-as-second-language (L2) learners to write a coherent argument. In our swiftly changing global society, students have to cultivate their skills to make a coherent and logical argument in order to convince others that their opinions are important, on top of having superb English language-command. This is vital for Japan (where I study) and other non-English speaking country to achieve a greater presence at the global stage.
