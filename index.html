Bio

My name is Jan Wira Gotama Putra. I am a PhD student at Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing Laboratory (自然言語処理研究室), Department of Computer Science (情報工学系), School of Computing (情報理工学院), Tokyo Institute of Technology (東京工業大学). I am currently working on automatic essay improvement system, supervised by Prof. Takenobu Tokunaga.

Contact: please email me at "gotama (dot) w (dot) aa (at) m (dot) titech (dot) ac (dot) jp"
