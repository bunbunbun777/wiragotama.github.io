Bio

My name is Jan Wira Gotama Putra. I am a master student at Computational Linguistics and Natural Language Processing Laboratory, Department of Computer Science, School of Computing, Tokyo Institute of Technology. My research interests lie in natural language processing, especially discourse analysis and text summarization. I am also interested in information retrieval and machine learning applications. I have published several papers, please check it out!

I have a personal side project creating a machine learning book in Bahasa Indonesia. If you are interested to read the book, please download it here, or you can download it through my blog. While the book is still premature, I hope it will be a good resource as an introductory material for studying machine learning. I will appreciate any review or comment.

If you wish to contact me, please email me at "gotama (dot) w (dot) aa (at) m (dot) titech (dot) ac (dot) jp". My laboratory is located at Tokyo Institute of Technology Ookayama Campus, W8E Building 6F (Tokunaga-lab).
